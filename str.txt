♠ SPADE (Physical Games – Moderate)
♠ 6 – “Moving Walls”

Players must cross a corridor while mechanical walls shift every 20 seconds.
Timing + sprint bursts required.
Failure = crushed or eliminated.

♠ 7 – “Weight Shift”

Floor tiles detect weight imbalance.
Players must distribute themselves strategically to move forward.
Wrong distribution → floor drop.

♠ 8 – “Limited Oxygen”

Players enter a maze with decreasing oxygen levels.
Find 3 checkpoints before timer runs out.
Encourages stamina + fast navigation.

♠ 9 – “Vertical Escape”

Climb a 6-floor industrial tower.
Randomized collapsing platforms.
Only top 40% survive.

♠ 10 – “Hunter’s Hour”

One AI-controlled hunter (or selected player) tracks participants.
Survive 60 minutes in a controlled zone.
Moderate brutality, not face-card level.

♣ CLUB (Teamwork Games – Moderate)
♣ 6 – “Signal Chain”

Players separated into rooms.
Only one can speak at a time through a relay system.
Solve a logic puzzle collectively.

♣ 7 – “Bridge of Trust”

Each player holds part of a rope system.
If one panics or lets go, whole team fails.
Encourages coordination.

♣ 8 – “Rotating Roles”

Every 5 minutes, leader role changes.
Task: transport object across obstacle field.
Tests adaptability.

♣ 9 – “Resource Divide”

Limited tools provided.
Team must choose who gets which equipment.
Wrong allocation → failure.

♣ 10 – “Sacrifice Ratio”

To unlock final door, team must decide how many members step into risk zone.
Mathematically survivable — but requires cold team decision.

♦ DIAMOND (Intelligence – Moderate)
♦ 6 – “False Majority”

Multiple choice puzzle.
Majority answer is always wrong.
Players must detect manipulation pattern.

♦ 7 – “Time Loop Logic”

Players repeat same room 3 times.
Each loop gives partial data.
Final answer requires combining loops.

♦ 8 – “Probability Chamber”

10 doors.
Survival odds displayed but misleading.
Players must calculate true probability.

♦ 9 – “Encrypted Exit”

Room filled with fake clues.
Only 20% clues are valid.
Pattern recognition required.

♦ 10 – “Game Theory”

Players must choose cooperate / betray options.
Balanced Nash equilibrium design.
Survival requires predicting others’ rational move.

♥ HEART (Psychological – Moderate)

Careful design needed here — moderate, not trauma-heavy.

♥ 6 – “Memory Betrayal”

Players answer questions about each other.
Wrong answers remove trust points.

♥ 7 – “Silent Accusation”

Players vote secretly who they suspect is “liar”.
No one is liar.
Game tests paranoia.

♥ 8 – “Emotional Trigger”

AI voice reveals personal secrets (fake ones generated by system).
Players must decide if info is true or trap.

♥ 9 – “Isolation”

Players placed alone in dark rooms.
Only one door opens every 10 minutes.
Patience vs panic test.

♥ 10 – “Last Connection”

Players paired randomly.
Only one of each pair can pass.
But choice is hidden — system decides based on sacrifice timing.
